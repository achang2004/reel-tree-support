ReelTree Support

Welcome to the support page for ReelTree. Here you will find answers to common questions and support resources for our app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I sign up?

A: Sign up with a unique username and a strong password that has at least one uppercase letter, one number, and one special symbol.

Q: What do I do with points?

A: Use points to unlock more short films to watch and share in order to inspire your own ideas!

Q: How do I get points?

A: You can get points by completing achievements (e.g., liking or commenting a certain number of times).
